I spent all my childhood summers in Greece with my family and had my cousins to play with. My best memories are those times.
Family vacations were great, but you know what was better.That’s right School trips and camping trips with our friends. We made unforgettable summer memories: the bus rides, trekking, and all kinds of adventure activities.
Summer brought with it our favourite iced treats. Whether it was the chilled gola with colourful syrups, classic kulfi, or modern ice cream, we just couldn’t have enough.
